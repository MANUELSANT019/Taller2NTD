Título

Occupational safety and hazards in university: The development and application of text categorization technology

Autores

Sharifah Mashita Syed-Mohamad, Mohamed Erfan Mohamed Siraj, Nur Hana Samsudin, Mohd Hafiidz Jaafar, Yulita Hanum P Iskandar

Resumen

Previous research in the education sector has shown that the list of known uncontrolled hazards is long and growing despite of an extensive amount of research conducted. Considering on how much important safety at campuses, our study focuses on digitalization of occupational safety in campuses. A system is proposed which provides a structured investigation of accidents and reporting. With the statistical visualization capabilities with the aid of keyword extraction algorithms, the university management can profile health and safety cases in the campus. The solution is potentially a crucial management tool for the university to keeping the campus safe and healthy
